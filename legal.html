This site loads fonts from Google Fonts. Google may log your IP address when fonts are requested. See Google Fonts privacy FAQ for details.

Analytics

Contact

Questions about privacy may be directed to connoreonwolfe@gmail.com.

Cookie Policy

Does this site use cookies?

This site itself does not set any first-party cookies.

Third-party cookies

Embedded YouTube videos may set third-party cookies from Google/YouTube when you interact with them (e.g., pressing play). These cookies may be used by Google to track viewing behavior across websites for advertising or analytics purposes. You can manage or block these cookies through your browser settings or a browser extension such as uBlock Origin.

Because this site uses YouTube's standard embed (not the privacy-enhanced mode), YouTube cookies may load even before you press play on a video. If this is a concern, you can disable third-party cookies in your browser.

How to control cookies

Most browsers allow you to view, manage, and delete cookies through their settings menus. Disabling third-party cookies will prevent YouTube from setting tracking cookies but will not affect the rest of this site's functionality.
